About toCollege

The college is keen on providing the best stimulating educational environment for its student.

The college was established in the academic year 1977/76, thus being the first college to be opened in southern Libya. The duration of the college’s study was eight semesters.

Our Mission & Goals

College Mission

Ensuring excellence in scientific research and employing it to serve the community. Upgrading society and spreading knowledge among its members. Effective partnership and cooperation with community institutions..

mission

From this standpoint, the college’s vision is to achieve leadership at the local, and social and media sciences. The college also seeks in its mission to prepare distinguished competencies in giving and creativity in the fields of specialization and enriching innovative scientific research supported by graduate studies programs and contributing to community service and building distinct community partnerships taking into consideration the comprehensive quality system and achieving accreditation.

the message.

Excellence in teaching and learning, scientific research and innovation, and community service, through the promotion of the principles of scientific analysis and creative thinking in a stimulating academic environment, participation in the production, development and dissemination of knowledge, and interaction with the local and international community

College Goals

To develop the spirit of research in the hearts of students, to cultivate a love for knowledge and to seek it..

The College is pleased to raise its website on the university's website with the International Information Network as a first step to link it with its local and international surroundings. As a nucleus of the university Sebha, the college intends to take a steady step in order to develop and keep pace with the process of scientific progress in the various branches of knowledge, especially the social and human sciences. This site will be useful to the pioneers of science and scientific research.

Dean OfCollege

Out of the vision, mission and goals from community service and scientific research, and in response to the thirst of the Libyan community for an institution that meets its requirements in further development by institutions and individuals, its various departments was established in a number of disciplines according to plans and curricula developed very carefully to meet the requirements of society on In particular, and Libya in general, in accordance with its vision, mission and goals, taking into account the need to work on the quality and development of programs in order to obtain academic accreditation, which has become a requirement not for itself but for its outputs.

College StrategicPlan

The development of a strategic plan comes in the context of directing towards establishing the scientific method for the future planning of the aspirations and objectives of the college and its departments, and bringing about a comprehensive change that raises higher education and scientific research to global levels capable of facing current and future challenges. The most important strategic goals that the college seeks to achieve can be summarized:

Upgrading the academic level (scientific - practical - research) for college students Reviewing and developing graduate studies programs Enhancing the skills of scientific research for college students

Development of scientific research in the college:

Create a library and a specialized scientific research database Promote specialized scientific publishing for faculty members Supporting the participation of faculty members in conferences and scientific meetings
