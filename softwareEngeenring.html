Department of Software Engineering

Find out the rest Collage specialties

Software Engineering specializes in creating electronics applications and systems, so it is one of the departments of the Faculty of Modern Engineering that is designed to meet the requirements of the modern technology era. Where it appeared with the emergence of computers and mobile phones, this diversity helped to develop different hardware systems and to invent modern mobile devices such as laptops, tablets and other devices.

The software also specializes in updating the coding of computer software to be of high quality and efficiency to meet the business need and help in effective management in various sectors and establishments. Software engineers also contribute to improving the efficiency of previously designed applications or designing new applications.


Academic content for Software Engineering major:

The duration of the study is 4 years for the undergraduate stage and the academic content in most Turkish universities includes a variety of study programs, knowing that the content may differ from one university to another, the student must verify the course description of the course description on the site of the university he wishes to study with before enrolling .



The first study year

  • The relationship of software to mathematics
  • Physics
  • General introduction to chemistry

The second study year

  • Algebra
  • Programming languages such as coding
  • Computer systems
  • Data forms
  • Software security
  • Software Engineering Analysis and Design
  • Data management systems

The third study year

  • Statistics
  • Software Project Management
  • Digital analysis
  • Architectural programming

Fourth study year

  • Non-technical aspects of engineering
  • Program and code validation
