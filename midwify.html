Department of Obstetric and Midwifery

Find out the rest collage specialties

Objective

Pepartment aimed to graduate a technical sub staff with scientific skills in the profession of midwifery and Obstetric nursing.

The Word Of The Head department :

Midwifery department offers advanced technical education by using modern techniques.
Teaching program offers complete scientific experience , adds the practical domain and studying to improve health care for mother and newborns during and after labor .

department Vision :

The study of midwifery branch for student begun in the study year 2009-2010 , the branch awards the graduate diploma degree in midwifery techniques after passing the graduation requirement , a success in the second stage and pass the summer training

Job description of graduate :

  • Provide nursing services for pregnant mothers during pregnancy (pre-natal)
  • Show all skills related to postnatal care and newborn care
  • Carry out normal deliveries and care for mothers and newborns during the postpartum period
  • The ability to apply information and advice on family planning and reproductive health problems
  • Submission of skills in health education, medical and nursing and counseling for pregnant women
