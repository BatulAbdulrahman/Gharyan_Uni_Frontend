Ocular Anatomy Course (MDAT 202)

This course includes a description of the shape and composition of the different areas and parts of the human body with the naked eye and the surface markings of the internal organs, the applied and clinical significance of this formation in addition to the visual dissection of the body's components.

Embryology Course (MDAT 205)

This course enables the student to get acquainted with the training stages of all the organs and body systems and the various birth defects that arise during the stages of training.

Course Histology (MDAT 207)

This course includes a description of the structure of the main body tissues, their locations, their functional adaptation, and knowledge of the histological composition of the various organs and systems of the body.

Neuroscience Course (Anatomy / Physiology) (MDAP 333)

It is a common course between the Department of Anatomy and the Department of Physiology and includes the study of histological structure and the anatomical description of the nervous system and its functions and its application to clinical cases.
