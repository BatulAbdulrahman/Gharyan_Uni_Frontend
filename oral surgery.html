Department of Oral surgery

Find out the rest collage specialties

Oral surgery is one of the oldest medical specialties that appeared thousands of years ago, and doctors and therapists used primitive tools to extract teeth and treat dental problems and mouth infections by extracting narcotic drugs and analgesics from plants and flowers and merging them with other formulations, and used during the treatment of roots or extraction of molars.

It is one of the medical specialties that is increasing in importance day after day, whether in the form of dental treatment or cosmetic. Where the reasons for people going to dentists vary, dentistry includes treatment of dental pain and gum infections, orthodontics, plastic surgery and oral and dental surgery. Dentistry also specializes in treating dental fractures resulting from accidents or injuries in sports competitions.

The importance of studying Oral Surgery

European universities, are distinguished by their interest in medical specialties, and their keen interest in adopting modern teaching methods that combine theoretical curricula and practical applications. Most universities include dental training modules and they are equipped with all the equipment and sterilization devices that students need, helping them in practice under the supervision of doctors and department heads.Universities also usually provide practical training opportunities outside the university campus in the university's hospitals and outpatient clinics or with which they contract or by helping students to obtain those opportunities by organizing job fairs.
