Our Mission & Goals

Vision.

The university should occupy its leading and prestigious position in the fields of higher education and community service, and be internationally recognized for the quality of its scientific and innovative outputs..

Mission.

Sincerity in words and action, commitment and credibility, equality and teamwork.

the message.

Excellence in teaching and learning, scientific research and innovation, and community service, through the promotion of the principles of scientific analysis and creative thinking in a stimulating academic environment, participation in the production, development and dissemination of knowledge, and interaction with the local and international community

Goals

- Preparing young cadres capable of creativity, innovation and self-learning.

- Providing the community with specialists and experts in various fields, taking into account the evolving needs of the labor market, within the framework of the state policy to develop human resources.

- The continuous renewal of the educational process at the level of the first university and graduate studies.

- Conducting theoretical and applied research and studies to serve the community and participate in finding scientific solutions to its social and economic problems.

- Linking various university research and studies with comprehensive development plans and the future vision of society.

- Participation in community service and development through direct and continuous interaction with its economic, social and cultural institutions and providing scientific and technical advice to these institutions, in a way that leads to society benefiting from the university's energies and capabilities.

- Developing human resources and increasing their efficiency through providing continuous education and training programs for all institutions of society and disseminating scientific and human culture among members of society.

Believing that education guards the country is better than an organized army. The University of Gharyan represented by all its employees works as a team and one hand for its students in terms of providing the appropriate environment for study and harnessing all its holders in order to serve the student and defend his rights and give the opportunity to delegate to study at home and abroad in multiple disciplines, and provide job opportunities as members of their teaching staff as well She paid attention to the members of the teaching staff in terms of implementing programs that would raise their efficiency and develop their skills and provide the possible benefits that the institution did not hold back to all its employees.
