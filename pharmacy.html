Therefore, the specialty of pharmacy requires a strong research study based on scientific research and the study of chemicals and various elements so that students have a strong knowledge of the drug components and their side effects and lists of medications for chronic and seasonal diseases and other other diseases.

The importance of studying pharmacy :

The importance of specialization is due to the fact that it is complementary to all medical fields where doctors diagnose patients, write the necessary medications and determine the amount that suits the age of each of them and take into account health conditions, and the role of the pharmacist is varied, as some of the graduates of the pharmacy college decide to work in pharmaceutical companies to install and improve drugs with more effective materials, and choose Others work in pharmacies to dispense prescriptions and provide patients with all the medications and other necessities they need.

The collage content of the pharmacy major :

The duration of study in Turkish universities is 5 years for the bachelor's level and the academic content in most Turkish universities includes a variety of study programs, knowing that the content may differ from one university to another, the student must verify the course description of the course on the university's website that he wants to study It out before recording

The first year

  • Physics and Biophysics
  • Introduction to chemistry
  • Biology, Molecules, and Genetics
  • First aid and basic medical skills

The second year

  • Analytical chemistry
  • Vital Statistics
  • Public Health
  • Pathology and epidemics
  • Clinical biochemistry

The third year

  • Pharmaceutical / Medical Chemistry (Part One)
  • Pharmacology (Part One)
  • Pharmacology (Part Two)

Fourth year

  • Clinical pharmacy
  • Drug therapy (part one)
  • Pharmacy administration
  • Radiation pharmacy, biopharmaceuticals, biopharmaceutical products
  • The economy of medicines

Fifth and final year

  • Improving medicines and generating drug revival
  • Drug interactions and patient safety
  • Promote health care
