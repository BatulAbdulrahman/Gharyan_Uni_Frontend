About management

Vision

Quality and excellence in providing services to students and graduates according to the latest systems, techniques and methods to make the university one of the most prestigious universities regionally and internationally.

mission

Exploiting all capabilities and information technology to meet the university's aspirations according to international standards.

Goals

- Spreading awareness of the regulations, study instructions and exams.

- The continuous application and development of academic regulations in order to ensure competitive university outputs.

- Complete the registration, acceptance and transfer procedures in accordance with the legislation in force.

- Work on applying smart management in the work of management.

- Propose programs and procedures for outstanding students.

Principles and values

Sincerity, perfection, transparency, cooperation, justice and equality.

Systems

Distance education system.

The system is concerned with monitoring the conditions of students enrolled in distance studies in terms of downloading and dropping materials and announcing the results.

Recording system.

The system specializes in issuing enrollment numbers for new students by registering on the site.
